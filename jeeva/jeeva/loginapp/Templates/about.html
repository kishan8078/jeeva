    About Us Impact

Over 1.7 billion people globally lack access to financial services, facing challenges in securing the resources they require. Jeeva, a nonprofit organization, is dedicated to promoting financial inclusion and fostering the prosperity of underserved communities.

Our approach involves gathering funds through crowdfunding for loans, unlocking capital for those in need, enhancing the affordability and quality of financial services, and addressing the fundamental barriers to financial access on a global scale. Jeeva's initiatives empower women to establish businesses, enable farmers to invest in essential equipment, and assist families in affording necessary emergency care.
